# C-NEWS  
**Community Narrative Early Warning System**

> A decision-support platform designed to provide early situational awareness of emerging online narrative trends through explainable, human-in-the-loop analysis.

---

## ðŸ§­ Overview

C-NEWS is a **preventive, awareness-oriented analytics platform** that helps institutions understand how potentially harmful narratives emerge and evolve in public online spaces.

The system is designed to support **early awareness and informed human decision-making**, not automated enforcement.

---

## ðŸŽ¯ Problem Context

Rapid dissemination of online narratives can sometimes contribute to:
- Social misunderstanding
- Community tension
- Misinformation amplification

Existing approaches are often reactive and lack early visibility into **how narratives form and spread over time**.

---

## ðŸ’¡ Solution Summary

C-NEWS provides:

- Narrative-level trend analysis  
- Early warning indicators (non-alarmist)  
- Explainable insights instead of black-box scores  
- Human-in-the-loop review workflows  
- Exportable summaries for reporting and briefing  

The platform focuses on **patterns and trends**, not individuals.
